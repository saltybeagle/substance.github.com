Create impressive web publications

Whether you're a journalist, writer or scientist, the Substance Composer
is the tool to create and share professional web-first publications.

Distraction-free structured writing

The Substance Composer lets you focus on the content, leaving the layout part to the authoring system.

Rich citations instead of just Hyperlinks

The Substance Composer supports academic guidelines for referencing external sources. You can describe resources (such as webpages) and reference them multiple times in your document.

Add remarks and corrections

Create notes while you are writing, and have your document reviewed by someone else using simple markers.

Easy Self-Publishing

Once done with writing your article, you can preview the resulting web publication and export it to a folder on your harddisk.

What's special?

A full fledged-editing environment for your desktop.

The Substance Composer is built with modern web-technology and ships as desktop application for OSX, Windows and Linux. Articles are written and securely stored locally, until they are turned into modern web publications.

Privacy and full control of your content

Substance gives you full control of your content. You can share your Substance documents on Dropbox or Google Drive for simplicity (if you trust them). Or you can use strong encryption, save your document on a floppy disk and store it in a vault.

Easy to integrate and customize

Substance's input and output formats shine because of its simplicity and interoperability. It is very easy to customize the look and feel of your publications and automate the publishing process.
