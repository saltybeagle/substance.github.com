How to test?

Hoo to test

Try to create real articles, publish them online and let us know. Tell us what you like about the interface and what you think could be improved. We have a very open ear to your feedback and it's very possible that good suggestions will make it into the final product.To get in touch, email us info@substance.io. Try to keep the mails short and informal. We'll be trying to respond as quickly as possible.

Read the user manual

Make sure you read the user manual. We'll keep that document updated to describe new features, known issues and point you to useful resources.

Donate

The Substance project is being developed since 2010 and has been, for the most part, a voluntary effort. Please support our work by considering a donation. In order to donate send us an email to contribute@substance.io , we'll get you an invoice and provide you with payment instructions.
